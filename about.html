A Few words about Me!

Born in Sherpur, Bangladesh, a young boy grew up in close connection with nature, observing the intricate balance of the environment. From an early age, he witnessed the detrimental actions of illiterate individuals who hunted wild animals and birds, causing significant harm to local wildlife. Distressed by these occurrences, he was driven by a deep sense of responsibility to protect nature and its inhabitants. He aspired to become a wildlife rescuer, dedicating his life to saving endangered species and preserving their natural habitats. Through years of dedication and training, he advanced in his career, ultimately becoming a Wildlife Ranger Officer. In this capacity, he successfully rescued and rehabilitated thousands of injured and displaced animals, working tirelessly to prevent poaching and illegal wildlife trade. His efforts not only saved countless animals but also contributed significantly to conservation awareness and the protection of biodiversity in his region. His commitment to wildlife conservation remains a testament to his passion and unwavering dedication to safeguarding nature for future generations.
